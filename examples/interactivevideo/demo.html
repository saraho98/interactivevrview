Desktop/laptop instructions

  • Play: Click the play/pause button, and you can seek using the timeline (make the window larger if the timeline is occluding the play/pause button).
  • Rotate scene: Click and drag over the video player.
  • Fullscreen: Click fullscreen icon in bottom right of the video player.
  • Active reorientation and hybrid modes only: While viewing the video, rotate the scene to the important point in the shot by pressing the "Switch Viewpoint" button.

Mobile instructions

First, open this page on your mobile phone and click the fullscreen icon on the video player. Then, turn the phone so that it is horizontal to the ground.
  • Play: Tap the top-middle of the horizontal screen.
  • Rotate scene: Change the orientation of the phone.
  • Active reorientation and hybrid modes only: While viewing the video, rotate the scene to the important point in the shot by tapping the top-middle of the horizontal screen.

Mobile w/ cardboard instructions

First, open this page on your mobile phone and then click the cardboard button in the bottom right hand corner of the video player. Put the phone in the cardboard player.
  • Play: Press the cardboard button
  • Rotate scene: Change the orientation of the cardboard player.
  • Active reorientation and hybrid modes only: While viewing the video, rotate the scene to the important point in the shot by pressing the cardboard button.
